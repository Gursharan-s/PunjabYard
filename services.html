Expert Solutions

Precision Services

PunjabYard supports farmers beyond equipment purchases by offering personalized mechanical assistance, flexible leasing solutions, and reliable regional support. Our goal is to ensure that every machine continues to perform efficiently throughout its lifecycle, helping farmers reduce downtime, improve productivity, and maximize the value of their investment.

FLEXIBLE TERMS

Seasonal Machinery Rental

RPunjabYard offers flexible machinery rental solutions designed for modern farming needs. From tractors and rotavators to combine harvesters and seeders, farmers can rent high-quality equipment during critical farming periods. Our transparent pricing, verified machinery, and flexible rental terms help improve efficiency while eliminating the financial burden of purchasing expensive equipment.

🔧
24/7 SUPPORT

On-Farm Maintenance

Our mobile service teams travel directly to your farm with genuine spare parts, hydraulic repair equipment, diagnostic tools, and experienced technicians. From routine servicing and emergency breakdown repairs to engine maintenance and performance inspections, we ensure your machinery stays in excellent working condition. Our fast response minimizes downtime, reduces repair costs, and helps you maintain uninterrupted productivity during every stage of the farming season.

🌐
GPS & TECH

Operator & Telemetry Training

Equip your machine operators with the knowledge and practical skills needed to operate modern agricultural equipment safely and efficiently. Our training covers GPS guidance systems, yield mapping, auto-steering technology, variable-rate fertilizer application, smart dashboard diagnostics, and routine maintenance practices. By improving operator confidence and technical expertise, we help maximize machine performance, reduce fuel consumption, minimize operational errors, and increase overall farm productivity throughout every growing season.

SCHEME GUIDANCE

Subsidies & Paperwork

Our financial advisory team assists farmers with every stage of the application process, from preparing essential documents and calculating return on investment (ROI) to submitting applications for government subsidies, agricultural grants, low-interest farm loans, and sustainable farming schemes. We simplify complex paperwork, ensure compliance with eligibility requirements, and help you secure the financial support needed to invest confidently in modern agricultural machinery and technology.
